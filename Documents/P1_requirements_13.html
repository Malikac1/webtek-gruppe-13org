Our client

Enzo Gorlomi of Papa's


Image source

Enzo Gorlomi is an Italian chef and owner of Papa's. Enzo has transformed the world of pizza with his passion and expertise.

Born in Naples, Enzo's culinary journey began in his grandmother's kitchen, and today, he stands as a master of Neapolitan-style cuisine.

With a dedication to authenticity and quality, Enzo has solidified his place as a beloved figure in the world of Italian cuisine.

Purpose and goals

The purpose and goals of Papa's is to inform new customers about Papa's; what Papa's is and what Papa's offers, as well as acting as a link between hungry customers and the cooks.

The website will show customers the rich menu and the golden history, and the cozy essence of Papa's through our hand-crafted design. We will have a dedicated page to Papa's menu, include Enzo's and Papa's lovely and rich backstory, as well as a contact form to get in touch with Papa's.

Audience

Papa's audience consists mainly of young adults, couples, and families looking for that little extra.

Users would want to find and read about menu items; their history, their allergens, their ingredients, and the price. Some users may want to contact Papa's; be it for reserving a table for their whole family, or asking for their lost jacket. Other users may want to read about Papa's history and the culture surrounding it.

Content and organization

Necessary pages and features
  • Landing page

    The landing page will be where our users' first impressions are made. Therefore, this page must encapsulate the essence of Papa's culinary pride.

    The landing page should allow for easy access to the other parts of the site, whilst also giving the user a glimpse of what's to see (and eat). The page should include images representing Papa's

  • Menu page

    This will be the "main attraction" on our site. It will show all menu items neatly sorted so the user can swiftly find what they are looking for, providing the same top-notch experience as Papa's. We have not set in stone how we will pursue this, but we will likely use a table, a list, or a flex grid.

  • About page

    The about page will tell the users about Papa's and Enzo and their respective culinary beginnings. Enzo's biggest inspiration is his grandmother, and the page should capture that. The page will include text and some images.

  • Contact us page

    The contact us page will let the user communicate with Papa's. We will use a form and the mailto protocol to satisfy this requirement.


Extra
  • When the user clicks on a dish, an info pop-up will show

Requirements

Functional requirements
  • The site should have a working "Contact Us" form that will send the restaurant management an email
  • The site should inform the user about the restaurant menu's options via a list or a table
  • The site should inform customers about the restaurant's opening and closing hours via a table
  • The site should make the user feel warm and welcome like the restaurant does

Non-functional requirements
  • The user should be able to find the menu item they are looking for within 15 seconds from entering the website
  • The user should easily, and at a glance, be able to find any allergens in any menu item
  • Every page must load in less than one second (not including images)
